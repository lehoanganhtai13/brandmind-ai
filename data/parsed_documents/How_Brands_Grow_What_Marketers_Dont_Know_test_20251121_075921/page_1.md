Imagine you are the Insights Director of Colgate Palmolive. Margaret, the Senior Category Manager for toothpaste, is standing at your office door and she is obviously distressed. She is waving a recently received report from your global market research supplier, and this is what it shows:¹²

**Figure 1.1:** Toothpaste brands: US market shares (volume)

This data presents a comparative analysis of market share between two toothpaste brands, Colgate and Crest, based on percentage of volume. Crest holds a significantly larger market share at 37% of the total volume, while Colgate accounts for 19%. This indicates a substantial difference in consumer preference or product distribution between the two brands, with Crest dominating the market volume compared to Colgate.

*Data source: Spaeth & Hess 1989.*
