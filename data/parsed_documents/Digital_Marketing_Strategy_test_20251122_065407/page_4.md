# Global strategy

Globalization can be a very challenging process for any organization. It can also take some companies by surprise as they outgrow their initial local plans. This brings cultural, language, process and many other challenges. With the growth of digital, almost all businesses have an international presence through the internet even if their target audience is very localized and so some aspect of global strategy should be included in almost every digital strategy – even if that aspect is to ensure that global presence is minimized.

## Culture

There are many cultural differences around the world, too many to count, and the relevant ones must be understood when building a global strategy. This can range from religious beliefs to manners and it is impossible to know what we don’t know – so doing your research is important here.

For example, did you know that Mexicans celebrate New Year’s Eve by eating 12 grapes at the stroke of midnight? That the colour red is lucky in China? That Suriname’s land is 91 per cent jungle whereas there are virtually no trees left in Haiti? These facts can affect your copy, conversations, brand and many other factors.
