## Online, Mobile, and Social Media Marketing  
### Skipping the Checkout Line

The convenience of running to the store for a few grocery items can be hampered by long checkout lines. The creative geniuses at Selfycart solved this issue by developing an app that allows shoppers to browse, scan, and pay for products in participating stores using their mobile devices without waiting in line.
