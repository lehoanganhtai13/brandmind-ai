## Online, Mobile, and Social Media Marketing  
### On the Move

According to Google’s Consumer Barometer, an interactive digital consumer insights tool, some 35 percent of UAE and Saudi Arabian under-35s go online using either smartphones or tablets. The 2016 data suggests that 30 percent of UAE residents prefer mobile devices compared to traditional desktops; in Saudi Arabia, this is 32 percent. In both countries, over 70 percent of smartphone owners prefer to carry out tasks, including product searches and purchases, using their smartphones. Despite some continued reluctance over online security and product quality, 24 percent of the population of the United Arab Emirates had recently made a purchase online.
