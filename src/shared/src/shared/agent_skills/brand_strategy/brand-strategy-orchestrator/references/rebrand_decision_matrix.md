# Rebrand Decision Matrix

## When to Use
Use this matrix in Phase 0 when the user has an **existing brand**. Score 6 diagnostic signals to determine rebrand scope.

## 6 Diagnostic Signals

Score each signal 0-2:
- **0** = No issue
- **1** = Moderate concern
- **2** = Severe issue

### Signal 1: Brand-Market Misalignment
**Question**: Brand positioning no longer fits market reality?
- 0 = Brand positioning still relevant to current market conditions
- 1 = Some aspects of positioning feel outdated or less relevant
- 2 = Market has shifted significantly, brand feels completely out of place

### Signal 2: Competitive Erosion
**Question**: Competitors have eroded your differentiation?
- 0 = Brand still clearly unique in its category
- 1 = Some competitors now offer similar value props
- 2 = No meaningful differentiation remains

### Signal 3: Audience Disconnect
**Question**: Target audience has shifted or brand no longer resonates?
- 0 = Strong emotional connection with target customers
- 1 = Connection weakening, some audience segments drifting
- 2 = Brand does not speak to current or desired customers

### Signal 4: Internal Fragmentation
**Question**: Brand identity inconsistent across touchpoints?
- 0 = Consistent brand expression everywhere
- 1 = Some inconsistencies (different menus, mixed visual styles)
- 2 = Fragmented — each location/channel feels like a different brand

### Signal 5: Reputation Damage
**Question**: Brand has negative associations?
- 0 = Generally positive brand perception
- 1 = Some negative associations or outdated perceptions
- 2 = Significant reputation issues that hinder growth

### Signal 6: Strategic Pivot
**Question**: Business model or strategy changing significantly?
- 0 = Business strategy stable and working
- 1 = Evolving — new offerings, new markets, gradual shift
- 2 = Major pivot — new concept, new audience, new business model

## Score Interpretation

| Total Score | Recommended Scope | What It Means |
|-------------|------------------|---------------|
| 0-3 | **Reinforce** | Brand fundamentals are sound. Focus on strengthening current positioning. |
| 4-6 | **Refresh** | Core brand is viable but expression needs updating. Keep strategy, refresh visuals and messaging. |
| 7-9 | **Repositioning** | Significant strategic gaps. Brand needs new target, new POD, or new value proposition. |
| 10-12 | **Full Rebrand** | Multiple critical signals. Comprehensive overhaul recommended — start fresh. |

## Agent Protocol
1. Ask the user each signal question naturally (not as a scoring exercise)
2. Score based on their answers and your research (Phase 0 context)
3. Present the total score and recommended scope
4. Explain the reasoning behind the recommendation
5. Get user agreement before proceeding with the recommended scope
