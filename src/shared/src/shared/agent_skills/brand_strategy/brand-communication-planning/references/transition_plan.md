# Transition & Change Management Plan — Rebrand Only

## When to Use
Only for **refresh**, **repositioning**, and **full_rebrand** scopes.
Skip entirely for **new_brand** scope (set transition_plan = null).

## Transition Approach

Choose one:
- **Big Bang**: everything changes at once on D-Day (higher risk, cleaner break)
- **Phased**: gradual rollout over weeks/months (lower risk, potential confusion)
- **Inside-Out**: internal first, then customer-facing (recommended for most F&B)

## 1. Stakeholder Communication Plan

Map each stakeholder group:

| Stakeholder | Impact Level | Communication Method | Timing | Key Message |
|-------------|-------------|---------------------|--------|-------------|
| Employees | High | Team meeting + brand kit | 2 weeks before launch | "We're evolving to..." |
| Loyal customers | High | Personal email + social | 1 week before launch | "Here's what's changing and why..." |
| Suppliers | Medium | Email notification | 1 week before launch | "Updated branding assets..." |
| Media/Press | Medium | Press release | D-Day | "Announcing our new brand..." |
| General public | Low | Social media + in-store | D-Day | Discovery through new experience |

## 2. Internal Rollout

Steps for internal brand adoption:
1. Leadership alignment session (ensure buy-in)
2. All-hands brand reveal meeting (share the why)
3. Distribute brand guideline kit (do/don't reference)
4. Role-specific training (customer-facing staff first)
5. Feedback loop setup (collect employee concerns)

## 3. Customer Communication

Define:
- **Announcement message**: what is changing and why (positive framing)
- **Channels**: social media, email, in-store signage, website banner
- **Tone**: confident, warm, inviting (not apologetic)
- **FAQ**: anticipate customer questions
  - "Why are you changing?" → "We're evolving to better serve you..."
  - "Is the quality the same?" → "Everything you love stays. We're adding more..."
  - "What about my loyalty points?" → "All rewards carry over..."

## 4. Physical Asset Changeover

| Asset | Current State | New State | Estimated Cost | Timeline |
|-------|--------------|-----------|---------------|----------|
| Exterior signage | Old logo/name | New identity | 5-15M VND | Week -2 to D-Day |
| Interior signage | Old branding | New branding | 3-8M VND | Week -1 to D-Day |
| Menus | Old design | New design | 2-5M VND | Week -1 |
| Packaging | Old cups/bags | New packaging | 5-20M VND | Order 4-6 weeks before |
| Uniforms | Old style | New style | 3-10M VND | Order 4-6 weeks before |
| Business cards | Old design | New design | 0.5-1M VND | Week -2 |

Adjust cost estimates based on number of locations and brand scope.

## 5. Digital Migration

| Platform | Changes Needed | Timeline |
|----------|---------------|----------|
| Instagram | New profile photo, bio, highlight covers, grid strategy | D-Day |
| Facebook | Cover photo, about section, profile photo | D-Day |
| TikTok | Profile photo, bio update | D-Day |
| Google Business | Name (if changed), photos, description | D-Day |
| Website | Full redesign or theme update | Week -1 to D-Day |
| Delivery apps | Logo, description, menu photos | Week -1 |

## 6. Transition Timeline

### Pre-Launch (4-6 weeks before)
- Finalize all brand assets
- Order physical materials (packaging, signage, uniforms)
- Brief internal team
- Prepare digital assets
- Schedule announcements

### D-Day
- Switch all digital profiles simultaneously
- Install new physical signage
- Launch announcement campaign
- Activate new packaging/menus
- Monitor social media reactions

### Post-Launch (2-4 weeks after)
- Collect customer feedback
- Address any confusion or concerns
- Fine-tune messaging based on reactions
- Complete any remaining physical changeovers
- First performance review (1-month check)

## 7. Risk Register

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Customer confusion | Medium | High | Clear announcement + FAQ + staff training |
| Negative reactions | Low-Medium | High | Proactive storytelling + rapid response plan |
| Delivery delays (signage/packaging) | Medium | Medium | Order early + have transition materials ready |
| Staff resistance | Low | Medium | Involve staff early + explain the why |
| Lost SEO/reviews | Low | High | Careful Google Business migration + redirect strategy |
